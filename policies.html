General Info and Policies


    Full Service or Staffed Events

  • Complete setup of food, including chafing racks and canned heat.

  • Appropriate serving utensils.

  • Heavy duty plastic plates coupled with high grade disposable silver colored plasticware wrapped in a dinner napkin with ribbon.

  • Cloth linens for food station table(s) and/or beverage stations.

  • Personalized table decorations for the food buffet station and table (based on season or client theme) with fresh floral arrangements.

  • Plastic drinkware for soft drinks and water if needed.

  • Complete breakdown and clean up of food areas/kitchen at the end of the function following event venue policies and procedures.

  • 18% Gratuity will be added to all events unless requested amount is different.


    • Drop Off Events

  • Delivery and set up of food, including wire chafing racks and Canned Heat( if ordered).

  • All appropriate serving utensils.

  • Reflective or Clear Plasticware wrapped in white napkin with black Band and tied with ribbon or Plastic-ware Packet; depending on the event.

  • Paper, styrofoam, or plastic plates (depending on orders).

  • Paper or plastic coffee cups for soft drinks, coffee, and water if needed.

  • $25.00 delivery fee


    • Important Reminders

  • Payment agreements are as follows: All events require a $300.00 deposit to confirm and save the date. When the date of the function is confirmed and menus are agreed upon. Scrumptious Catering requires a fifty percent non-refundable deposit thirty days prior to the event with the balance due prior to day of the event. We gladly accept payment in the form of cash, check or Credit Card.

  • Cancellations of the event must be made seventy-two hours prior. Orders cancelled less than forty eight hours prior to the event will result in a 100% charge to the client. Everything cancelled in the 30 days prior to deadline will result in a 50% charge to client. Inclement weather or other cancellations will be handled on a case by case basis.

  • If you have any questions, comments or concerns, please do not hesitate to call or email. Thank you in advance for allowing us to submit a proposal. If Scrumptious Catering is your chosen caterer, you will not be disappointed as we strive to make every event “Scrumptious”!
